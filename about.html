About the Neon Lounge!



Established in 2010 Neon High Lounge has been a mainstay in the SoCal bud scene. From championship strains to celebrities requesting our services for open bud bars, the Neon has been filling SoCal shoes for a while. We also will be starting a YouTube Channel soon.


All of our products pass California state testing requirements and specifications. We enjoy these products ourselves too so we wouldn't put just anything into our bodies. With that in mind we buy natural and organic only.
